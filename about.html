About Us

El Maaly for Educational Projects (E.M.E.P) is a forward-thinking educational holding company that owns, manages, and transforms international schools across the MENA region.

Founded in 2011, with headquarters in Alexandria, Egypt, we are the driving force behind some of the nation’s most prestigious schools:

  • GEMS Academy Alexandria
  • Wellington International School
  • Wellington International Nursery
  • Antoniadis International School
  • Antoniadis International Nursery
  • Energize Future Child Care - KSA

Our strength lies in our dual capacity. We operate both as school owners and as a comprehensive educational organization that designs, governs, and elevates academic and operational systems to international standards.

Building on this strong foundation, E.M.E.P is now expanding into Saudi Arabia with remarkable speed and vision. The first E.M.E.P Energize Future Child Care in Riyadh officially opened in November 2025, followed by the launch of three international schools in Riyadh and Jeddah in September 2026.

At E.M.E.P, we believe education is more than academics. It is about building futures, shaping communities, and preparing students to thrive in a globalized world.
